    Who we are?:

    Welcome to Brother's Barber, a family-owned barbershop with over 30 years of experience. Established in 2013 near Fiskepiren Bay in Stavanger, we take pride in serving a diverse clientele from various countries in this oil capital city of Norway. Our reputation is built on providing quality cuts at affordable prices, making us a popular choice for students, adult to retirees. Join us for a welcoming and skilled haircut experience.
